20 Years of the Unheard Third Survey: A Look at Hardship Data for Low Income New Yorkers

This is a map demostrating the level of hardships for low income New Yorkers based on the UHT data over the past 20 years (2002-2021).

There are four hardship categories examined in the data: housing, medical, food, and economic.

The data displayed in this map only reflects respondents who experience a moderate to severe level of hardship for each category.

The data in this map comes from the Community Service Society of New York's annual survey of low income New Yorkers called the Unheard Third Survey.
